Atlas Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026 | Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Atlas Tracker stores all your data locally on your device.
We do not transmit, share, or sell your data to anyone. Period.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Atlas Tracker, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Dose Logs: Compound names, dosage amounts, units, timestamps, injection sites, and personal notes
  • Side Effects: Self-reported health effects associated with doses
  • Weight Entries: Body weight measurements and associated notes
  • Tracking Preferences: Dosing schedules, notification times, and reminder settings
  • App Preferences: Weight unit preference (lbs/kg), notification settings, biometric lock preference

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

If you choose to connect Atlas Tracker to Apple Health:

  • Weight Data (Read): We can read weight entries from Apple Health to display your weight history
  • Weight Data (Write): We can save weight entries you log to Apple Health

We do not access any other HealthKit data types. We cannot read your heart rate, blood pressure, steps, sleep, medications, or any other health metrics.

Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID:

  • Atlas Tracker never receives or stores your biometric data
  • Authentication is handled entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave
  • We only receive a success/failure response from the system

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Camera or photo library access
  • Contact information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or crash reports
  • Any data from third-party SDKs

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Core Data: Encrypted local database for dose logs, compounds, weight entries, and tracking data
  • UserDefaults: App preferences (encrypted by iOS)
  • Apple Health: Weight data (if you grant permission)

Encryption

Your data is protected by:

  • NSFileProtectionComplete: AES-256 hardware encryption
  • Data is only accessible when your device is unlocked
  • Encryption keys are managed by Apple's Secure Enclave

No Cloud Storage

  • We do not sync your data to any cloud service
  • We do not use iCloud, Firebase, or any backend servers
  • Your data never leaves your device (except Apple Health integration you control)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  1. Display your dose history and tracking information
  2. Send local notification reminders (if enabled)
  3. Calculate statistics and visualizations within the app
  4. Export your data to CSV format (when you initiate it)
  5. Sync weight data with Apple Health (if you enable it)

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, user profiling, sale to third parties, or any purpose beyond app functionality.

Data Sharing

We Do Not Share Your Data

Atlas Tracker does not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to advertising networks, analytics providers, third-party services, social media platforms, healthcare providers, insurance companies, government agencies, or any other entity.

Apple Health Integration

If you enable Apple Health integration, weight entries you log may be shared with Apple Health. This data is then governed by Apple's privacy policies. You control this integration and can revoke it anytime.

Data Export

You can export your dose history to CSV format. This file is created locally on your device and is shared only when you explicitly choose to share it.

Your Rights and Controls

Access Your Data

  • View all your data within the app at any time
  • Export your complete dose history to CSV format

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual dose logs, weight entries, or tracked compounds
  • Reset all app data from Settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Control Permissions

  • Enable or disable Face ID/Touch ID at any time
  • Enable or disable notifications at any time
  • Connect or disconnect Apple Health integration
  • Choose discreet notification mode to hide compound names

Data Retention

  • App Data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Apple Health Data: Managed separately by Apple Health (persists after app uninstall)
  • Notification Data: Stored temporarily in memory until delivered
  • Export Files: Stored in temporary directory until you share or delete them

Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Hardware Encryption: All data encrypted with AES-256 via Apple's Secure Enclave
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • No Network Transmission: Zero attack surface from network vulnerabilities
  • No Third-Party Code: No external SDKs that could compromise security
  • Sandboxed Storage: iOS app sandbox prevents unauthorized access

Children's Privacy

Atlas Tracker is not intended for use by children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app's content is appropriate only for adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and providing notice within the app for significant changes.

International Users

Atlas Tracker stores all data locally on your device. Your data remains on your device in your jurisdiction. No data is transferred internationally. Local data protection laws apply to your device storage.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt-out. Since we do not collect, share, or sell personal information to third parties, most CCPA provisions are not applicable.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process data based on your consent and contract performance. You have rights to access, erasure, portability, and restriction. Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, we are not a data controller under GDPR for external processing.
