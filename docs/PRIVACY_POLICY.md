# Atlas Tracker Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

## The Short Version

Atlas Tracker keeps all your data on your device. We don't collect it, we don't see it, we don't sell it. Your health tracking stays private.

---

## What Data Atlas Tracker Collects

### Data You Enter

| Data Type | Purpose | Stored Where |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Compound/medication names | Track what you're taking | Your device only |
| Dosage amounts | Log your doses | Your device only |
| Dosing schedules | Remind you when to take doses | Your device only |
| Injection sites | Track site rotation | Your device only |
| Side effects | Monitor your response | Your device only |
| Notes | Your personal notes | Your device only |
| Weight entries | Track your progress | Your device only |
| Inventory counts | Track your supply | Your device only |

### Data From Apple Health (Optional)

If you grant permission, Atlas Tracker can:
- **Read** your weight data from Apple Health
- **Write** weight entries you log to Apple Health

This lets your weight data sync across health apps. You can revoke this permission anytime in Settings > Health > Atlas Tracker.

### Preferences Stored

| Setting | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Weight unit preference (lbs/kg) | Display weights in your preferred unit |
| Notification settings | Whether and when to send reminders |
| Biometric lock setting | Whether Face ID/Touch ID is enabled |
| Onboarding completion | Skip intro screens after first use |

---

## What We DON'T Collect

Atlas Tracker does **NOT** collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number
- Your location
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any data that identifies you

We have **no servers**. We run **no analytics**. We use **no third-party SDKs** that collect data.

---

## Where Your Data Lives

**All data stays on your device.**

Atlas Tracker uses Apple's Core Data framework to store information in a local database on your iPhone. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Never syncs to any cloud service
- Never gets sent to us or anyone else
- Is encrypted when your device is locked (NSFileProtectionComplete)

---

## Face ID / Touch ID

If you enable biometric lock:

- Atlas Tracker uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework
- Your biometric data (fingerprint, face scan) is **never** accessed by the app
- Apple's system handles authentication securely
- The app only receives a yes/no answer: "Did the user authenticate?"
- No biometric data is stored, transmitted, or accessible to the app

---

## Apple Health Integration

### What We Access

Atlas Tracker requests permission to read and write **body weight data only**. No other health data types are accessed.

### How We Use It

- **Reading:** Import weight entries from Apple Health to display alongside your tracking
- **Writing:** Save weight entries you log in Atlas Tracker to Apple Health

### Apple Health Data Rules

Per Apple's requirements:
- We do **NOT** use HealthKit data for advertising
- We do **NOT** share HealthKit data with third parties
- We do **NOT** sell HealthKit data
- HealthKit data is used solely to provide app functionality

### Revoking Access

You can revoke Health access anytime:
1. Open iPhone Settings
2. Tap Health
3. Tap Data Access & Devices
4. Tap Atlas Tracker
5. Toggle off permissions

---

## Data Security

### Encryption

Your data is protected by:
- **Device encryption:** All data is encrypted when your device is locked (NSFileProtectionComplete)
- **Secure enclave:** Face ID/Touch ID data is protected by Apple's secure hardware

### No Network Transmission

Atlas Tracker makes **no network requests**. Your data cannot be intercepted because it's never transmitted.

---

## Your Rights

### Access Your Data

All your data is visible within the app. You can view your complete history anytime.

### Export Your Data

Atlas Tracker includes a data export feature. You can export your dose history and tracking data in standard formats.

### Delete Your Data

To delete all Atlas Tracker data:
1. Delete the app from your device
2. Your data is permanently removed

Or within the app:
- Delete individual dose logs, compounds, or entries at any time
- All deletions are permanent and immediate
